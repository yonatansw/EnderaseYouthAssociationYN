Enderase Volunteers network

Introduction Enderase (Amharic ‘እንደራሴ’) used to express shared experience or understanding with someone else. It suggests that the speaker can relate to the feelings, thoughts, or experiences of another person because they have gone through something similar. Enderase is a way to show solidarity and support for others.
