About Us

Enderase Youth Assocation

Introduction Enderase (Amharic ‘እንደራሴ’) used to express shared experience or understanding with someone else. It suggests that the speaker can relate to the feelings, thoughts, or experiences of another person because they have gone through something similar. Enderase is a way to show solidarity and support for others.

Our Mission

As Youth led assocation we have mission statement of enlightening,empowering and exposing the untapped potential of the youth to the next level in value of love,diligence and excellence.

Creating an enviroment for the youth where the youth engagemnt is secured and advancing thier most potential to the maximum level.

Equibing the emerging youg leaders across the country from rural up to urban in love and diligence where sustanavlity and development is acheived.

Our Vision

Enderase has vision of light which we want to see a youth generation that is eye opened and be light his nation & generation.

This is Vision of Light were we open the eyes of the youths to see what they have and work on their full potential in changing thier community and genration.

Our vision is a heart born sight were we speak what we see and were we do what we speak.Enderase youth assocation has been dedicated since the offical opening time up to now in making this to happen.
